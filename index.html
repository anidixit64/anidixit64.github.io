About Me

Aniket Dixit

I am a senior at the University of Michigan pursuing a B.S in Computer Science and Linguistics. My interests include machine learning, natural language processing, database management, and backend development. I have programmed in Python, C++, and SQL, with experience in Tableau, Docker, PyTorch, and Flask. I am also a passionate researcher with a specialization in historical linguistics. I am currently in the process of writing my thesis on Latin language contact in the early Roman Empire.

In my free time, I am a fervent soccer player, language learner, and Civil War buff.
